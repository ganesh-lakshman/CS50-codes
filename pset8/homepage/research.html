RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

The main purpose of this research is to reduce road accidents using Artificial Intelligence in vehicles. Our idea is to develop a sensor in a vehicle which automates the rules to be followed while driving. Specifically the research questions addressed in this study are: 1)What are the main causes for an accident to occur? 2)How many articles have been published on using Artificial Intelligence in vehicles? Knowing answers to our questions in the primary research we came to know different causes for road accidents to occur and on which perspective we have to develop our project. Through the secondary research different articles related to our project are collected from different Database like science direct, google patents , IEEE explorer etc.Most of the articles were collected from IEEE explorer.The following table shows article count and percentage extracted from different Database.
